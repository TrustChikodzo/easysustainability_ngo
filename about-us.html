WHO ARE WE?

Our mission is to fill the gap between education and green jobs
Easysustainability is a project-based NGO operating at the European level, with the mission to upskill youth through non-formal educational methods, project works and MOOCs for them to have higher chances to land green jobs. This way, Easysustainability aims to make green know-how more accessible also to the general population. Through its projects, which have as protagonists associates and volunteers who want to upskill themselves, Easysustainability promotes the adoption of more sustainable and circular solutions in each area of society (industry, academia, nonprofits and more). As an environmental sustainability-focused NGO, we are committed to finding innovative solutions to the world's most pressing environmental challenges and will design our projects on this basis. Our area of action is incredibly vast: from sustainable entrepreneurship to pro-bono consulting projects to firms and other bodies that want to become more sustainable.

« Making sustainability mainstream through youth protagonism »

Our team is dedicated, diverse and multidisciplinary
After the last round of recruitment in January, our team reached a headcount of about 60 young professionals, graduates, and PhDs in STEM subjects, law, business, economics, humanities, and more. All candidate members come from diverse backgrounds and experiences with NGOs, public bodies and sustainable businesses (Cyrkl, ESGtree, 4goodcause, and more). They live across multiple EU countries, speak diverse languages and pertain to different ethnicities, as inclusivity is one of our core values.
