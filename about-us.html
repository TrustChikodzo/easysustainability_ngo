WHO ARE WE?

Easysustainability is an NGO project, founded in 2023, that aims to promote sustainability and related topics in each area of our society. The goal is to make knowledge more accessible to society as a whole, especially to the most disadvantaged ones.

We are a team of young professionals, graduates and PhDs currently working with several projects, which include webinars, pro-bono sustainability consulting for firms, Erasmus+ training courses.

« Making sustainability mainstream through youth protagonism »

If you feel passionate about sustainability and/or related topics, want to build a strong network of people working in this field, and also would like to cross-learn different aspects of it, feel free to join us and become a volunteer!

We will adopt an open approach to this project, changing it every time we need.
